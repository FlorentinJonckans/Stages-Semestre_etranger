Testimony of a student : Nicolas Juckel

In which countries would you like or have you been in ? If yes, why ?

  • I went to Japan during 3 months to do my internship in 2016. I decide to go there because I like the culture, the language ...

What have you prepared in your luggages before you go ?

  • I've prepared a toilet kit (gel, shampoo, toothbrush, flannel ...). Take care at the airport, some objects aren't allowed ! Cloths, my electronic appliances (computer, mobil phone, photo appliance, charger ...)

Which languages have you used during your internship ?

  • During my internship, I've mostly used english and thereafter, I've had to speak japanese or even french.

Have you met any financial issues ?

  • I benefited of the Japanese Stock Exchange of a value of 80 000 yens/month,wheter 600 euros given on my own hands by a member of this japanese school as well as the Blériot Stock Exchange(who is arrived after my internship).

Which relationships have you had with students and teachers ?

  • I've had a very good relationship with the teachers or the students, "Japanese people" have been very welcoming as soon as I arrived. I didn't expect to have such a very good warm welcoming.

Have you had some issues to find a housing ?

  • I was living in the school's dormitory for around 80 euros per month.

How did you live there ?

  • I had to adapt to the japanese student live. Thanks to the help of scholarships , I've learned that we can't live a "french life" in Japan because the price of the french's food are expensive. Meanwhile, I still visited the country in addition to my internship.

Have you met any other various issues ?

  • Apart from the language barrier with older people who didn't speak english or french I didn't have any issues
